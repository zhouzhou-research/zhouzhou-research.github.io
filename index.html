Welcome!

I am a PhD candidate in Digital Financial Technology at the National University of Singapore.

My research focuses on the impact of technology and information on financial markets. I combine Generative AI, machine learning, and alternative data to explore questions in Empirical Asset Pricing and Sustainable Finance. My work aims to understand how digital transformation reshapes the information environment for investors and firms alike.

Research Interests

  • Empirical Asset Pricing
  • FinTech
  • Climate and Sustainable Finance
